BobCat General Commands.

  • b!help - List of BobCats Commands
  • b!afk - Sets your AFK
  • b!ping - Shows BobCats latency
  • b!dead - Shows a dead chat message
  • b!diceroll - Rolls a dice of 1-6
  • b!coinflip - Lets Flip a Coin Heads/Tails
  • b!suggest [command] [reply]- Sends your command suggestion
  • b!hug [member] - Hugs a member u mentioned
  • b!stats - Shows the bots stats


  • b!avatar - Shows the bots stats
  • b!stats - Shows the bots stats

BobCat Moderation Commands.

  • b!clear [number] - Deletes a number of messages in a channel
  • b!kick @user [reason] - Kicks the member you mentioned
  • b!ban @user [reason] - Bans a member from the server
  • b!unban @user - Unbans a member from the server
  • b!mute @user [reason] - Mutes the member you mentioned
  • b!unmute @user - Unmutes the member you mentioned
  • b!slowmode [seconds]- Sets the channels slowmode
  • b!poll [message]- creates a poll This pings @everyone
  • b!givaway [mins] [prize] - Creates a giveaway
  • b!createemoij [url] [name] - Creates a giveaway
  • b!lockdown - Locksdown the channel
  • b!unlock - Unlocks the channel

BobCat User Commands.

  • b!avatar - Shows your profile pic
  • b!userinfo - Shows infomation on you


BobCat Server Commands.

  • b!membercount - Shows the membercount for the server
  • b!servericon - List ofservericon
  • b!serverbanner - Shows the server banner
  • b!serverinfo - Shows info about the server
  • b!roleinfo - Shows info about a server role




BobCat Money Commands.

  • leaderboard - Shows you the leaderboard
  • balance - Shows somes balance
  • shop - Shows whats in the shop
  • work - Gives you money
  • withdraw - Withdraws money from your bank
  • deposit - Deposit money into your bank account
  • send - Sends money to people
  • rob - Takes money from people
  • buy - Buy things from the shop
  • sell - Sell your items
