BobCat Music General Commands.



Defult prefix: =

  • join - Joins the current voice channel your in
  • leave - Joins the current voice channel your in
  • play [song] - Plays the song you requested
  • pause - pauses the current song
  • resume - Resumes the current song
  • stop - Stops the current song
  • skip - skips the current song to the next
  • queue - Shows the song queue
